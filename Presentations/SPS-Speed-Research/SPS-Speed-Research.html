How many games of chess exist?

The actually number has not been computed but there are a few estimates.

Shannon's Number: $10^{120}$

On average, a player has 30 moves and a game last about 40 turns \begin{equation} (30)\times(30)\times(30)\cdots =(30)^{80} \approx 10^{120} \end{equation}
Approximately $10^{53}$ atoms in the universe.

How Many Sensible Games?

Assume players only have 3 "sensible" moves per turn. \begin{equation} (3)\times(3)\times(3)\cdots =(3)^{80} \approx 10^{40} \end{equation} Every human playing unique games of chess a day, it will take
100 quadillion Galactic Years to play all games.
