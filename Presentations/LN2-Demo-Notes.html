LN2 Demo notes

  1. Introduce yourself and school
  2. Introduce Liquid Nitrogen
    1. Query: What is air made of?
  3. Plastic Cup
    1. Show audience
    2. Pour some on floor
    3. Query: What is fog/smoke?
    4. Point out nitrogen is clear (like air)
  4. Tea Kettle
    1. Query: Is LN2 hot or cold?
    2. Phase change. Liquid â†’ gas (x 1000 expansion)
  5. Thermometer
    1. Describe daily temperature (freezing to boiling)
    2. Stand on chair and drop remainder of thermometer
  6. Flower
    1. Get volunteer
    2. Put two flowers in, wait for boiling to subside
    3. Let volunteer smash one flower
    4. Save second flower and let it defrost
  7. Match
    1. Query: When match is brought close to LN2 it will ...
      1. go out
      2. stay the same
      3. burn brighter
      4. explode
    2. Bring match near dewar
    3. Query: Why did it go out?
  8. Prep Balls
    1. Bounce balls and place in dewar
    2. "Ring" cup and place in dewar
  9. Balloon
    1. Get volunteer
    2. Query: What will happen to balloon in LN2?
    3. Let volunteer place balloon in dewar
    4. Query: Why did it shrink? Where did the air go?
    5. Compare reexpanded balloon to wilted flower.
      Comment on crygenic freezing
  10. Film Canisters
    1. Query: What is most dangerous aspect of LN2?
    2. Remind audience about expansion rate
    3. Fill bottle (about half way) with LN2
    4. Slam cap on (gloved hand) (not too tight)
    5. Explain phase transition and factor of 1000 expansion rate
  11. Ball Conclusion
    1. ping pong
    2. bouncy
    3. racquet (it will shatter)
    4. Ring the bell (support on ladle handle and hit with tongs)
