PQ: Bowling Ball Acceleration

A 10kg bowling ball is 100 meters above the Earth's surface. What is Earth's acceleration towards the bowling ball?

PQ: Bowling Ball Acceleration (sol)

$$Ma=G\frac{Mm}{(R+100)^2}$$ $$a=G\frac{m}{(R+100)^2}$$ $=1.6\times10^{-23}$m/s$^2$

PQ: Moving Earth

Some people have proposed tackling climate change by pushing Earth further away from the Sun. How much energy would it take to move Earth 1 meter further away from the Sun? 1AU=1.5E11 meters.

PQ: Moving Earth (sol)

$$W=-\Delta U=GM_sm_e\left(\frac{1}{AU}-\frac{1}{AU+1\text{m}}\right)$$ $$W=3.5\times10^{22}\text{J}$$

For comparison, global energy consumption in 2021 was about 400 exajoules .

$$\frac{3.5\times10^{22}\text{J}}{400\times10^{18}\text{J/yr}}=87.5\text{yr}$$
