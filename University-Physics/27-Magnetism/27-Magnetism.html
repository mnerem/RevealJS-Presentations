27: Magnetism

Earth's Magnetic Field

Earth has a magnetic field that we
roughly estimate to be a bar magnet.

$|\vec{B}_\text{Earth}|=[25,\,65]\mu$T

Earth's Moving B-field

Earth's magnetic field undergoes a reversal anywhere between
0.1 and 50 million years.

Earth Pole Terminology

  • (A) Geographic Poles
    A1 - North Pole
    A2 - South Pole
  • (B) Geomagnetic Poles
    B1 - North Pole
    B2 - South Pole
  • (C) Magnetic Poles
    C1 - South Pole
    C2 - North Pole
