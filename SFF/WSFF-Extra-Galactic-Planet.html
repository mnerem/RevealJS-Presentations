Weekly Science Fun Fact

Extra-Galactic Planet

Chandra X-ray Observatory

  • Identified with NASA's Chandra X-ray Observatory
  • Launched in 1999: flagship X-ray astronomy satellite

Evidence for Extra-Galactic Planet

  • Furthest planet ever found: 28 million light-years
  • Seen as temporary dimming of X-rays from binary star system.
  • Dimming caused by planet passing in front of an X-ray source around a neutron star or black hole orbiting a companion star.
